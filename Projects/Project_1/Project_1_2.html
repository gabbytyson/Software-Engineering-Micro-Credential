Financial Aid

Explore NYC financial aid options: Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) for federal aid. Apply for state aid through the New York State Tuition Assistance Program (TAP). Check with your college for institutional aid. Visit NYC's Financial Empowerment Centers for guidance on managing finances and student loans.
External Scholarship Aid

Reduces financial burden, enhance affordability, and reward academic merit, empowering students to pursue education without excessive debt.

External Scholarship Aid

Maximize NYC scholarship opportunities: Research local organizations, foundations, and businesses offering scholarships. Utilize online platforms like Fastweb and Chegg for external opportunities. Tailor applications to showcase achievements and community involvement. Stay proactive, meet deadlines, and seek guidance from school counselors for a successful scholarship search in the city.

Competitions

Seize global scholarship opportunities: Explore renowned competitions like the Rhodes Scholarship or Fulbright Program. Thoroughly research eligibility criteria and application requirements. Showcase academic excellence, leadership, and community impact. Cultivate strong recommendation letters. Plan ahead, meet deadlines, and pursue your global academic aspirations with determination.
